# Initial Concept

Create an AI-driven labor compliance platform for film & TV productions. The project is a client-side React application built with Vite, utilizing the Google GenAI SDK for AI capabilities. The app is associated with an AI Studio project.

---

# Product Definition: ByTheBook AI Studio App

## Vision
ByTheBook is an AI-driven labor compliance platform designed to navigate the complexities of film and TV production labor agreements. It provides production teams with immediate, accurate, and actionable insights into union rules and compliance status.

## Target Audience
The platform is built for the key stakeholders responsible for the financial and legal health of a production:
- **Production Managers & Line Producers:** To ensure production stays within budget and complies with labor laws.
- **Labor Compliance Officers:** To monitor and enforce adherence to complex union contracts.
- **Accountants & Payroll Staff:** To accurately calculate wages and avoid penalties associated with compliance errors.

## Core Goals
- **Automated Union Rule Interpretation:** Leverage AI to interpret and apply complex rules from major unions such as SAG-AFTRA, DGA, and IATSE.
- **Precision and Traceability:** Provide verifiable, citation-backed answers that link directly to specific sections of union agreements, ensuring every claim is auditable.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Proactively identify and reduce compliance risks and potential penalties, ensuring productions run smoothly and legally.

## MVP Features
- **AI-Powered Advisor:** A conversational interface that provides instant, cited interpretations of specific rules, linking directly to the source text via an integrated document viewer.
- **PDF Upload & Analysis:** The ability to upload union contracts, timecards, and other documents for automated AI analysis and data extraction.
- **Compliance Dashboard:** A centralized view providing real-time status updates, compliance alerts, and health indicators for the entire production.

## Design Principles
- **Clean and Professional:** An enterprise-grade aesthetic that builds trust and conveys reliability.
- **Simple and Intuitive:** A user experience designed for efficiency, ensuring that non-technical users can easily navigate complex compliance data and AI features.
